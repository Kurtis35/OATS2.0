❓ Frequently Asked Questions

What should I wear in Elgin weather?

Elgin has a Mediterranean climate. Bring layers as temperatures can vary. In summer (Dec-Feb), expect warm days and cool evenings. In winter (Jun-Aug), pack warm clothing for chilly mornings and evenings.

Who do I contact in an emergency?

For transport emergencies, call our 24/7 hotline: +27 79 503 6849. For medical emergencies, dial 10177. Your accommodation will also have emergency contact information.

When should I be ready for shuttle pickup?

Please be ready 5 minutes before your scheduled pickup time. Our drivers will wait for a maximum of 10 minutes. Check the status banner above for any updates to shuttle times.

Are rural roads safe at night?

Yes, our professional drivers are familiar with all local routes and drive well-maintained vehicles. We monitor road conditions and adjust routes as needed for your safety and comfort.
